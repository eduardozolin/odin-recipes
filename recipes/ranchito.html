El Ranchito Salad

A very tasteful salada made with lettuce, carrot, cheese and a mix of spices, sided with chicken stripes

Ingredients

  • 1 Carrot
  • American lettuce
  • Red cabbage
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Doritos
  • Chicken breast
  • Salt, lemon, garlic and pepper

Sauce

  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 spoon of white wine vinegar
  • 1 spoon of barbecue sauce
  • Parsley
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • One lemon
  • 200gr of heavy cream

Steps

  1. Mix together all the ingredients of the sauce
  2. Chop the vegetables and mix it together
  3. Grate the cheese and fry the bacon. Also cook the chicken breasts and slice them.
  4. Mix all the sauce, the vegetables, the cheese and the bacon; add the chicken stripes and Doritos.
